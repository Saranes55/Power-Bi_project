# PowerBI_Projects
Welcome to my **Data Analytics Portfolio**, a collection of end-to-end analytics projects built by using **Power BI**. Each project focuses on solving real-world business problems through structured data analysis, insightful KPIs, and interactive dashboards.

This repository demonstrates my ability to:
- Understand business requirements
- Clean and model data
- Perform exploratory and KPI-driven analysis
- Build interactive and decision-focused dashboards
- Present insights in a clear and business-friendly manner

## Projects Overview
## 1. Attrition Analysis
**Domain**: Human Resources Analytics.
**Objective**: Identify key factors influencing employee attrition and retention.

Key Focus Areas:
- Attrition rate by department, gender, age group
- Job role and experience impact on attrition
- Salary, work-life balance, and satisfaction trends
 
Business Value: Helps HR teams reduce employee turnover by identifying high-risk segments and key drivers of attrition.

## 2. Bank Customer Analysis
**Domain**: Banking & Financial Services.
**Objective**: Analyze customer behavior and identify churn patterns.

Key Focus Areas:
- Active vs inactive customers
- Credit score and balance analysis
- Product usage and customer segmentation
 
Business Value: Supports banks in improving customer retention strategies and targeting high-value customers effectively.
